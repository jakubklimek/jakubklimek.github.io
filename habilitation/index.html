Jakub Klímek - materials for habilitation

Linked and Open Data researcher and practitioner

Applicant introduction

Jakub Klímek has been an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, Charles University, Prague, Czechia since 2017. He has also received his Ph.D. degree here in 2013.

His research interests include data interoperability, open data, linked data, and the semantic web. He is the author of 11 papers in journals with an impact factor and over 50 papers in peer-reviewed conference proceedings.

He participated in multiple international research projects, including the EU FP7 project LOD2 - Creating Knowledge out of Interlinked Data and the EU FP7 project COMSODE, both dealing with developing tools for processing Linked Data and applying them to real-world datasets, as a regular project participant, the EU H2020 project OpenBudgets.eu, creating linked data based specifications, tools and transformations for budget and spending related data, as a work package leader responsible for the specifications, and the EU CEF Telecom project STIRData, dealing with linked data based specifications, tools and transformations in the domain of company data, as the principal investigator for Charles University.

He served in program committees of international conferences, including the International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC), the International Conference on Knowledge Engineering and the Semantic Web (KESW), the International Joint Conference on Knowledge Discovery, Knowledge Engineering and Knowledge Management (KEOD) and in two instances of the World Wide Web (WWW) workshop LDOW: Linked Data on the Web. He also reviews papers for journals, including the Semantic Web Journal, ACM Computing Surveys, Expert Systems with Applications, IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computing, etc. Jakub has been invited to speak at international venues such as the Semantic Interoperability Community (SEMIC), the European Data Summit or the conference ENDORSE - The European Data Conference on Reference Data and Semantics follow-up event: Linked Data platforms in and across the EU Member States.

Jakub has also participated in multiple contracted research projects for the industry, including a linked data-based project for an energy distribution company e.on, a linked data implementation project for the Slovak Ministry of Investments, Regional Development and Informatization of the Slovak Republic, and a project funded by the Wikimedia Foundation allowing users to load linked data directly to Wikidata.

In addition to the academic part of his career, Jakub has been working for the Ministry of the Interior of the Czech Republic since 2015 in the role of a linked and open data expert. He is an author of a number of data specifications ensuring open data interoperability, he is the technical lead of the Czech National Open Data Catalog and the Czech Open Data Portal, publishing best technical practices for publishing open data and linked data in the context of a public administration.

Jakub contributed to web standards such as the DCAT W3C Recommendation, or DCAT-AP and StatDCAT-AP, its European Application Profiles.
